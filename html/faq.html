Botox

Most clients report little to no discomfort during the injection procedure, no more than a small pinch. We do offer topical numbing if you are anxious about the discomfort factor.

Botox takes between 24-72 hours to see first results. Full results can be observed at around fourteen days.

Since it may take up to 14 days for Botox to show its full effect on you, we recommend a period of 14 days before further sessions to prevent facial asymmetry.

You can go directly back to your day-to-day activivities following a treatment session. However we recommend avoiding excessive physical activity and applying make-up over the treated area for the day.

Results will begin to fade in 3-4 months after the initial treatment.


Lasers & IPL

Lasers produce a single, concentrated wavelength of light while IPL produces a wide spectrum of between 550nm and 1200nm to specifically target melanin in the hair.

You would need between 6 to 12 treatments to achieve permanent hair reduction results.

Treatments are spaced 4 to 6 weeks apart to target follicles during the growth stage.

We recommend areas like underarms, bikini lines and legs for women and backs and necks for men.

Avoid picking or scratching the treated skin. Do not use any other hair removal methods or products, other than shaving, on the treated area during the course of your laser treatments, as it will prevent you from achieving the best results.


Anti-Cellulite & Tummy Tucks

While the fat eliminated from the body will not return, parts of the body that haven’t been treated can develop fat tissue, so be sure to continue practicing a lifestyle with a healthy diet and exercise to maintain results.

You may start seeing results in as little as three weeks, but the most dramatic results will occur around two months after treatment. The fat will be gradually flushed out of the body for as long as four to six months.

You may notice some redness, swelling, bruising, tingling, and other skin sensitivity in the treatment area. All are temporary and will go away in a few days or weeks.

Since this is not a surgical procedure, you will be able to return to your regular activities right away.

The length and number of treatments will depend on what you decide with your clinician during your consultation. Upon your first visit, we will talk with you to put together a customized plan that will address your needs and get you the results you want.
